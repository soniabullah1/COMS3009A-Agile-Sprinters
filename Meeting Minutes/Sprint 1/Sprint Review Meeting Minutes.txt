Agile Sprinters (Group 8)Minutes of the First Sprint Review MeetingMonday, 12 April 2021 (10:00 – 10:16):The First Sprint Review Meeting with the Client, Development Team and Scrum Master was held online on 12 April 2021 at 10:00 a.m.Attendance:o Client: Mr Rylan Perumalo Scrum Development Team: Abdullah Haffejee, Bhavik Govan, Colin Hugo, Jemma Sundelson, Kayla Levy, Rushil Patel, Tristen Haverlyo Scrum Master: Sonia BullahReview:The project was presented to Mr Perumal at the start of the meeting. The presentation included showing and describing the process of signing-up and signing-in to the application as well as showing an example of what a user’s chat list may look like. Feedback:Mr Perumal suggested that a list of all previous messages exchanged be present above the typing section found on the chat tab and also reiterated that a user should only be able to see their messages – not messages belonging to other users and their contacts. Adjournment:With there being no further questions, the meeting was adjourned at 10:16 a.m.
